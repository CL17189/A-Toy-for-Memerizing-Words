



Welter and bombast were two words that were constantly being used by the kneads. Surreptitious and tantamound were two other words that were used by the squeamish. Frothy, historionic, and amorphous were all words that were used by the unrequited. Quibble and slake were two words that were used by the crutch. Crux and debase were two words that were used by the expedite. Nettle and discursive were two words that were used by the harbinger.
