

I was feeling groggy after my morning coffee when my wife started in with her usual repartee. I knew I was in for a long day when she started talking about her gargantuan shopping list. I half-listened as she went on about aspersions and mites, respite and bonhomie, finicky and usurp. I knew the denouement would be her importuning me to take her to the store, so I adulated her stentorian voice and mongrel dog. I was in for a long day.
