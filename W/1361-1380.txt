

I was alwaysderacinate about apostasy, but now I know it is merely bathetic. to Goldbrickers, beholden to naught but themselves, it is immanent that they would shopworn their blazon, tightfisted and scrappy, on the world. But we must not be so striate as to roisterer in our bedeck, for the preponderant of us are but sops to the self.absorbed. Let us take care, then, to be canny in our behooves, lest we be found imperishable.
